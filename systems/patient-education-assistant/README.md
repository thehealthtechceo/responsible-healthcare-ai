# Patient Education Assistant

The Patient Education Assistant is a non-clinical, patient-facing AI system designed to provide general health education.

This system explicitly prevents diagnosis, treatment, medication advice, or clinical decision-making. It is intended as a demonstration of Responsible AI principles across the full AI lifecycle in healthcare.

## Intended Use
- General health education
- Preventive care awareness
- Health literacy support

## Out of Scope
- Diagnosis
- Treatment
- Prescriptions
- Emergency medical advice
